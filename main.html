Nestled along India's spectacular west coast, Goa is a tropical paradise blending Portuguese colonial charm with pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage. This former Portuguese enclave offers sun-kissed shores, historic churches, spice plantations, and world-class seafood, making it the ultimate holiday escape for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike.
