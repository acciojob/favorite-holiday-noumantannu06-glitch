Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Nestled in the Himalayan mountains, Manali offers breathtaking views, adventure sports, and serene apple orchards. This popular hill station attracts nature lovers with its snow-capped peaks, gushing rivers, and traditional Pahadi culture throughout the year.

Top Attractions

  1. Attraction :Experience the thrill of Rohtang Pass with panoramic mountain views
  2. Attraction :Visit the ancient Hadimba Devi Temple surrounded by cedar forests
  3. Attraction :Adventure at Solang Valley for paragliding and zorbing
  4. Attraction :Explore the scenic Vashisht Hot Springs with healing sulphur waters
  5. Attraction :Trek to the serene Jogini Waterfall through pine forests

Must-Try Foods

  • Food 1: Siddu - steamed bread stuffed with local herbs and ghee
  • Food 2:Dham - traditional multi-course Pahadi thali feast
  • Food 3:Chana Madra - tangy chickpea curry cooked in yogurt
  • Food 4:Apple Cider - refreshing local drink from Manali's famous orchards
  • Food 5:Trout Fish Fry - fresh river fish cooked with Himalayan spices
