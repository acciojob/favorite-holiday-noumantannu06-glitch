Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Nestled in the Himalayan mountains, Manali offers breathtaking views, adventure sports, and serene apple orchards. This popular hill station attracts nature lovers with its snow-capped peaks, gushing rivers, and traditional Pahadi culture throughout the year.

Top Attractions

  1. Rohtang Pass: Experience the thrill with panoramic mountain views.
  2. Hadimba Devi Temple: Visit the ancient temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  3. Solang Valley: Adventure awaits with paragliding and zorbing.
  4. Vashisht Hot Springs: Explore the scenic springs with healing sulphur waters.
  5. Jogini Waterfall: Trek to the serene waterfall through pine forests.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu: Steamed bread stuffed with local herbs and ghee.
  • Dham: Traditional multi-course Pahadi thali feast.
  • Chana Madra: Tangy chickpea curry cooked in yogurt.
  • Apple Cider: Refreshing local drink from Manali's famous orchards.
  • Trout Fish Fry: Fresh river fish cooked with Himalayan spices.
